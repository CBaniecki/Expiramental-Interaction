See, Touch or Immerse Yourself in Music

We developed Sensability to expand access to music therapy to everybody-regardless of sensory impairments. We have a multisensory approach to music and musoc therapy. Why focus on audio when music can be physically and visually immersive? Sensibility expands music from a purely audio experience to a visual augmented and tactile experience.

Sensability Features

Music Visualization

Colored visuals captivate the attention of the hearing impaired and are used as communication in the place of sound.

Augmented Reality Experience

The engaging visuals in the Augmented Reality environment corresponding with the touchable aspect would have the concept of connecting with sound.

Tactile Experience Subscription

Why a tactile object?

Music isn't purely an audio experience. You can feel vibrations from the sound. We transformed the vibrations from sound into a physical object. By turning the sound into a physical object, it is almost as though the sound becomes tangible.
