The purpose

The purpose of this is to create an accommodating experience for individuals with impairments. This project is specifically focused on the hearing impaired, but with the physical aspect a visually impaired individual could use the tactile aspect as well. By having visualizations associated with the music and a tactile association, music can be touched and seen instead of being heard.

Why would a tactile object be necessary?

The inspiration for this idea is the way the hearing impaired comprehend music through/by feeling the vibrations within the sound itself. By turning the sound into a physical object, it is almost as though the sound becomes tangible. Colored visuals captivate the attention for the hearing impaired, and are used as communication in the place of sound. The engaging visuals in the Augmented Reality environment corresponding with the touchable aspect would have the concept of connecting with sound.
