Music for all senses

Sensability Rehabilitition is inclusive products designed to give individuals with impairments the ability to comprehend music universally.

Experience Music from a Visualized Perspective

Using p5.js and the sound library in p5.js, this music visualization was created. The visualization is created from the amplitude of the music. The goal of this visualization is to use the piano instrument as music therapy to feel music for the hearing impaired.

Internet accessibility

This extension is the music accommodation aspect of the brand application. With the addition of an accessibility profile feature in Google Chrome sound will become a visual image. All you need to get started is an email address, it's that simple.
